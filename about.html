WHO WE ARE

We are Italian wedding planners based in Venice and together with you we can create authentic weddings in Venice. We provide expert advise for any aspect of your wedding planning in Italy: such as dealing with the never ending bureaucratic paperwork, sourcing local trustable suppliers (venues, photographers, flowers…) and also giving you creative ideas with an Italian

Getting married in Venice is not complicated, but it could be a hassle if you do not know the language or you are not familiar with the Italian culture. For these reasons we can help you in many ways. So do not hesitate to contact us and tell us your wedding idea – we will give you initial suggestions which will reflect your style as well as your budget
